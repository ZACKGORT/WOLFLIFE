Wolfgang & Co · wolfie.love Made
with care. We build the digital presence of places and people who believe in craft. Not templates. Not shortcuts. Things that are worth visiting, worth using, worth coming back to. We work closely with the people behind them. "Every product we build is something we'd want to use ourselves. That's the only bar we know how to set." Wolfgang & Co is a small studio. We take on a handful of clients at a time and go deep on each one. The result is work that knows what it is and why it exists. Five products. One ethos.

wolfie.love The

Every client we work with gets access to the same design system, the same editorial approach, the same obsessive attention to how things feel when you actually use them. Commerce 03 Shop Retail experience · objects worth owning
Wolfie Shop Only
what lasts. Shop is a commerce experience built for makers and merchants who have something worth selling. Not a storefront — a presentation. The difference is in every detail: how a product enters the frame, how it's described, how it moves when you get close to it. We believe buying something should feel like a decision, not a transaction. Shop makes space for that. The store is the brand. Every element is considered. Nothing is filler. ForIndependent makers · boutique retail · curated collections Accessshop.wolfie.love · partner storefronts
Place 04 Real Estate Property presence · spaces worth finding
Wolfie Real Estate A place
you remember. Real estate is where the physical and digital meet hardest. Events 05 Invite Event presence · experiences worth attending

We build the page that makes people feel something before they even arrive. The best events have a point of view before they begin. Invite gives that point of view a place to live. RSVP, details, the story of why this thing is happening — all of it, considered. ForWeddings · brand events · openings · private gatherings Accessinvite.wolfie.love · per event
Hospitality 06 Food Restaurant presence · kitchens worth knowing
Wolfie Food Come
to the table. Food is where Wolfgang & Co began. The belief that a restaurant's digital presence should feel like the restaurant itself — dark, warm, considered, alive. The menu, the tap list, the reservation — all of it in the same voice as the room. We've built for tap rooms, kitchens, and wine bars. Each one a different room, the same commitment. If you've eaten somewhere that felt like it knew exactly what it was, there's a chance we helped it get there. ForRestaurants · breweries · wine bars · hospitality groups Accessfood.wolfie.love · live menus · operator dashboards "Hospitality is an act of imagination.
